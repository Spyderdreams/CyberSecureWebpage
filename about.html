About Jason McDaniel

Systems Engineer & IT Leader

With over 20 years in the technology industry, Jason McDaniel is a dedicated Systems Engineer and an experienced IT Leader. He has served in multiple roles, from Service Delivery Manager to IT Systems Engineer, building a wealth of experience in managing global support teams and enhancing IT security.

As a veteran who proudly served as a paratrooper in the U.S. Army, Jason values resilience, discipline, and teamwork. His passion lies in helping small businesses and entrepreneurs protect their digital assets by providing clear, actionable cybersecurity guidance.
